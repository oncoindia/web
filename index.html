A little about us

OnCo is an online application that helps students who are preparing for different exams including IIT JEE( mains and advanced ), board exams, different state level competitive exams, exams in which PCM is given.

The services of this app are totally free of cost. You can ask doubts, take free test series, counseling, and some more features. If you don't want to avail these features, you can refer our app to someone who needs them as all the services are FREE OF COST!

We want to provide free and qualitive education for all

Details

We are now collaborating with schools for organizing School Online Test Series and some more facilities. In this program, we will organize a free test for students of class 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th. They can give it on the OnCo app itself.

Test will organize on every Sunday. Generally preferred timing is 12:00pm to 03:00pm.

If the participation from any institute / organization / school / college is more than their 33.33 % , then we will also conduct motivational seminars ( currently online seminars ). In this session we will also distribute prizes to best performer of your school for each class.

Certificate to all participants will be send to their respective mail ids.
